The origin

Before kingdoms.
Before civilization.

Before kingdoms. Before technology. Before civilization — there was only instinct. The Primals are descendants of an ancient species caught between two worlds, neither apes nor human.

They began to evolve. Some embraced intelligence. Others clung to strength. Some sought knowledge hidden beyond the known world. Others believed the old ways should never be forgotten.

As generations passed, the Primals spread across a wild and untamed realm. Their bodies changed. Their beliefs changed. Their purpose changed. Yet one truth remained: They were unfinished.

The awakening

Something dormant
has begun to stir.

Every Primal carries traces of what they were and hints of what they may become. Their appearance reflects this struggle — part ape, part human, shaped by adaptation and their environment.

No one knows what caused the Awakening. Some believe it came from the stars. Others claim it emerged from deep beneath the earth. Many think the truth has been lost forever.

Now a new era approaches. Ancient symbols are resurfacing. Forgotten relics are being discovered. Strange markings have begun appearing across the world — as if something dormant has started to awaken once again.
